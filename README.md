# Customer Value Segmentation

## Status

🚧 **Ongoing Project** – currently under development.
This project focuses on identifying **high-value customers, loyal vs. discount-driven buyers, promotion dependency, and retention opportunities** for a D2C fashion brand using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

---

## Overview

This project analyzes customer transactional and behavioral data of a **Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) fashion brand** to understand what drives customer value, loyalty, and repeat purchases. The objective is to move beyond basic sales reporting and build a structured customer analytics framework that helps the brand identify its most valuable customers, evaluate discount dependency, and improve retention strategy.

The project aims to answer business questions such as:

* Who are the genuinely loyal customers vs. discount-driven buyers?
* What customer behaviors indicate long-term value?
* How much revenue depends on promotions?
* Which customer segments, product categories, and geographies should the brand focus on?

---

## Dataset

* **Source:** D2C Fashion Brand Customer Dataset
* **Format:** CSV
* **Records:** ~3,900 customers
* **Domain:** Retail / Fashion / Customer Analytics

The dataset contains customer-level transactional and behavioral information used for segmentation, loyalty analysis, discount dependency analysis, and business insight generation.

---

## Business Problem

The brand has customer data but lacks a structured way to understand **who its best customers are, what drives loyalty, and how much current revenue depends on promotions**. Without these insights, marketing and product decisions are driven more by assumptions than evidence.

This project is designed to use available customer and transactional data to:

* identify **high-value customer segments**
* distinguish **loyal customers from discount-only customers**
* analyze **promotion dependency**
* uncover **category and regional growth opportunities**
* support a **data-backed retention strategy**

---

## Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* Segment customers based on value, purchase behavior, and discount usage
* Identify loyal customers vs. promotion-driven customers
* Measure revenue dependency on discounts and promotions
* Analyze customer behavior across product categories, geographies, and demographics
* Build an ideal customer profile for retention and acquisition strategy
* Generate actionable business recommendations to reduce discount dependency without hurting sales

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Loading

* Import dataset into Python
* Inspect shape, columns, and data types
* Perform initial data quality checks

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyze customer spending patterns
* Explore discount usage and promo behavior
* Study category-level and region-level performance
* Visualize customer distribution and purchase trends

### 3. Data Cleaning

* Handle missing values and duplicates
* Standardize column names and categorical values
* Correct data types and validate records

### 4. Feature Engineering

* Create customer-level metrics such as:

  * total spend
  * average order value
  * purchase frequency
  * discount usage rate
  * category diversity
  * customer value indicators

### 5. Customer Segmentation

* Segment customers into business-relevant groups such as:

  * High-Value Customers
  * Loyal Customers
  * Discount-Driven Customers
  * Moderate-Value Customers
  * Low-Engagement Customers

### 6. SQL Analysis

* Write analytical queries to explore:

  * customer purchase behavior
  * category contribution
  * promotion dependency
  * regional and demographic insights
  * revenue concentration across segments

### 7. Dashboard & Reporting

* Build a Power BI dashboard with:

  * KPI cards
  * customer segment analysis
  * revenue and discount insights
  * category and regional trends
* Summarize findings into a report/presentation
